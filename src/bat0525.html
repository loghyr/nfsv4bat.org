Overview

Oracle is pleased to host the Spring 2025 NFS Bake-a-thon in Ann Arbor, Michigan. This event combines in-person and virtual presence using peer-to-peer VPN connections.

  • Where: The Even Hotel in Ann Arbor

    Book your room now at the Bake-a-thon venue hotel or stay at a location nearby.

  • When: May 12 - 16, 2025

Testing Focus

Testing at this spring's event will include:

  • RPC with TLS
  • NFSv4.2 extensions
  • pNFS with Flex File layouts
  • Kerberized NFS
  • NFS/RDMA on iWarp

Registration

Send a notice of your plan to attend in-person to BAT Contact. Virtual attendees may gain access to our VPN by registering at Spring 2025 Registration.

Here are the steps to access the network:

  • Download the tailscale bits
    • Tailscale Download
  • Register with the Bake-a-thon VPN server
    • tailscale up --login-server https://headscale.nfsv4.dev
  • The registration will give an authentication link
  • Use that link to
    • Create an account on the BAT server
    • Authenticate your machine[s]
  • The command 'tailscale status' will show hosts that are accessible on the VPN
